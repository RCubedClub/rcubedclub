About R Cubed

Rcubed is a technical club with an emphasis on the practical aspects of computer science and technology. Its a place for like minded geeks to discuss and collaborate on all things tech.
So what are you wating for? Put on your building caps on and join us!


Our Values


MISSION: To instill an environment of technical mindset, skills and a love for all things tech among budding engineers.


VISION: R Cubed was aimed to motivate budding engineers to get their hands into practical implementation. Theory does not make an engineer, putting that theory into practice does. We aim to achieve that through collaborative projects, hackathons, seminars, contests and a lot more.
This is a wonderful time for technology and we aim to tap that opportunity to create an army of practically sound and enthusiastic individuals.
